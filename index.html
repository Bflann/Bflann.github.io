Brendan Flannagan

I am a student at the University of Michigan pursuing a degree in computer science and cognitive science. I am working toward my goal of becoming a video game developer. This site has examples of games that I have designed either by myself or in a group.

Flickernaut

Flickernaut is a stealth-puzzle game developed by Lights Out Studios. The game follows a stranded astronaut who must progress through puzzles which can only be solved by teleporting to a dark, dangerous, alien-infested dimension and lighting torches whose power help clear the way in the light dimension.
